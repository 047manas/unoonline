How to Play UNO!

  • Match Cards: Match by color or number/symbol.
  • Action Cards:
    • Skip: Next player loses turn.
    • Reverse: Changes play direction.
    • Draw Two (+2): Next player draws 2 cards (Stackable!).
  • Wild Cards:
    • Wild Card: Choose the next color.
    • Wild Draw 4 (+4): Change color and next player draws 4 (Stackable!).
  • Stacking: You can play a +2 on a +2 (or +4 on a +4) to pass the penalty!
  • Calling UNO: Press the UNO! button when you have 1 card left.
  • Catching Others: If someone forgets UNO, press Report! to penalize them!
